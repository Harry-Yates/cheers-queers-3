about

CHEERS QUEERS is a Hyper Island Student Project that came to life within the UX Design & Development module of the Digital Creative class of 2023. The brief was to create a tool using HTML, CSS and Javascript that makes our every day lifes easier.

CHEERS QUEERS was created by Rufus Glaser, Lisa Nagano Holm and Laura Popp, who came up with the idea of an interactive map of Stockholm displaying all the queer-friendly spaces the city has to offer. The aim is to make it easier for the LGBTQ+ community to find places where they feel welcome and safe.
